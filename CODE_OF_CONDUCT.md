# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community and projects will experience an environment free of harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Using welcoming and inclusive language**
- **Being direct but professional** in code reviews and discussions
- **Focusing on what is best for the community**
- **Showing empathy towards other community members**
- **Giving credit** to others' contributions and ideas
- **Providing constructive feedback** that helps others improve

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (doxing)
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, etc.
- Any form of unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Violent threats or language directed against another person
- Deliberately disrupting conversations or community spaces

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document the incident** (what happened, when, who was involved, if possible)
2. **Report privately** to project maintainers
3. **Do not engage** further with the person if it's safe to do so

### Investigation

Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All involved parties will be treated with respect and confidentiality.

### Consequences

Depending on the severity, consequences may include:

1. **Warning** - Private communication about the behavior
2. **Temporary ban** - Temporary removal from community spaces
3. **Permanent ban** - Removal from the community and project

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (repositories, issues, discussions)
- Community communications (email, chat, forums)
- Official project events
- Any representation of the project in public spaces

## Contact

If you witness or experience a violation:

- **Email**: Project maintainers (see README for contact info)
- **GitHub**: Private message to maintainers
- **Discussions**: Use private message feature

Responses to reports:

- Initial response: Within 48 hours
- Investigation: Within 7 days
- Resolution: As appropriate to the situation
